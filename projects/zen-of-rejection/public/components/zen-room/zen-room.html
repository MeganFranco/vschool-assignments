Meditation

Meditation has many health and physical benefits. It's a great way to either begin or end your day, The Daily Zen has a great 10 step guide beginners. You should also check out his post Meditation for Skeptics

My favorite meditation is the Metta Meditation, which can be a bit challenging. However, it only takes about 25 minutes. below are the steps:

  1. Visualize yourself
  2. For five minutes project, loving kindness towards yourself.
  3. When your mind wanders, repeat the mantra "May I be well, May I be happy"
  4. For the next five minutes, visualize someone in your life who you greatly respect and care for.
  5. For five minutes, project loving kindness towards this person.You can do this however you like, but many envision projecting light from themselves to the person they're visualizing.
  6. When your mind wanders, return your thoughts to that person, think "may they be well, may they be happy"
  7. Now visualize someone that you don't have any strong feelings towards. This is usually someone that you see every day, but don't really know. Think of the cashier in your cafeteria or the barista you see every morning when you get your coffee.
  8. Repeat steps five and six.
  9. Now for the hard part: envision someone with whom you've had issues.
  10. Repeat steps five and six.
  11. Visualize yourself and the three people you visualized standing in a circle, and project loving kindess around the circle. For the last five minutes, visualize the light of loving kindness expanding infinitely outwards.
